# SOP: Preventive Maintenance Service and Calibration

Purpose:
To maintain equipment accuracy, reduce downtime, and protect client investment.

PMS frequency:
- Surveying equipment: every 6 months
- Materials testing equipment: every 3 to 6 months depending on usage
- Utilities field equipment: every 3 months or based on field usage
- Fleet/mobile equipment: every 1000 engine hours or every 3 months, whichever comes first

PMS procedure:
1. Verify client and equipment serial number.
2. Review last service log.
3. Check physical condition.
4. Perform calibration or performance test.
5. Replace or recommend worn parts.
6. Update service log.
7. Recommend next PMS date.
8. Send service report to client.

Urgent PMS triggers:
- PMS overdue
- unstable readings
- engine hours above 1000
- visible cable wear
- warranty-sensitive equipment
