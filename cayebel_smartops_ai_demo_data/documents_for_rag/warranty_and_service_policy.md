# Warranty and Service Policy

Warranty coverage:
- Manufacturing defects within warranty period
- Software or firmware issue not caused by misuse
- Factory-related component failure

Not covered:
- Physical damage from mishandling
- Water damage
- Unauthorized repair
- Damage from incorrect usage
- Lack of required PMS when maintenance is mandatory

Service priority:
1. Active service contract clients
2. Warranty-sensitive cases
3. Equipment down / cannot operate
4. Regular PMS clients
5. Non-contract walk-in requests

Warranty claim requirement:
The client must provide:
- Equipment serial number
- Purchase record
- Service history
- Photos or diagnostic report
- Proof that required PMS was followed, if applicable

Recommended response:
"We will review the equipment history and warranty coverage first, then advise if the issue qualifies for warranty service or paid repair."
