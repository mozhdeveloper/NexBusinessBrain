# Sales Scripts

For PMS offer:
"Based on your equipment history, your unit is already due for preventive maintenance. Regular PMS helps avoid downtime, accuracy problems, and warranty issues."

For inactive client:
"We noticed your team has not scheduled service recently. Would you like us to check your equipment status and recommend the next maintenance schedule?"

For high-value quotation:
"To help your team finalize the decision, we can arrange a short technical consultation and walk you through the best configuration."

For GPS tracking:
"If your equipment is used in the field, GPS and engine-hour monitoring can help you know when service is needed before breakdown happens."
