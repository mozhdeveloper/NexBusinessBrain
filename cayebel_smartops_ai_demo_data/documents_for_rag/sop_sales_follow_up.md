# SOP: Sales Follow-Up

Purpose:
To make sure all leads, quotations, and active deals are followed up on time.

Follow-up rules:
1. New inquiry must be contacted within 24 hours.
2. Quotation must be followed up within 3 business days.
3. If quotation has no reply after 7 days, mark as high-priority follow-up.
4. If a deal is in negotiation for more than 10 days with no activity, manager review is required.
5. High-value deals above PHP 500,000 must have at least one follow-up every 5 business days.

Recommended sales message:
"Hi [Client Name], I am following up regarding the quotation we sent for [Equipment/Service]. Would you like us to schedule a short technical consultation to help finalize the best option for your team?"

Manager action:
Review overdue follow-ups daily and assign priority calls.
