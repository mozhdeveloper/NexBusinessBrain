# Cayebel SmartOps AI Demo - Company Profile

Cayebel-style company profile for demo purposes only.

The company supplies and services professional equipment for:
- Surveying departments
- Materials testing laboratories
- Utilities infrastructure teams
- Construction and engineering companies
- Equipment rental and field operations teams

The business provides:
- Equipment sales
- Preventive Maintenance Service (PMS)
- Calibration
- Technical service and repair
- Service packages
- Client support
- GPS / fleet tracking integrations
- Online PMS booking and payment options

Main operational pain points:
- Sales teams need better follow-up visibility.
- Service teams need quick access to PMS procedures and equipment history.
- Clients need visibility on their equipment, PMS schedule, and service history.
- Managers need to know which deals are stuck and which clients need attention.
- Technical documents and manuals are scattered.
