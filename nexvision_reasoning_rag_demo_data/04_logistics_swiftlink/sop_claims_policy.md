# SOP: Claims Policy

Claims eligibility:
1. Lost parcel: eligible if marked lost by warehouse or dispatch.
2. Damaged parcel: eligible if reported within 24 hours of delivery with photos.
3. Delayed express delivery: eligible for service fee refund review if delayed more than 48 hours.
4. Wrong address given by sender: not eligible.
5. Prohibited item: not eligible.

Approval rules:
- Claim below PHP 3,000: Claims Supervisor
- PHP 3,001 to PHP 20,000: Operations Manager
- Above PHP 20,000: General Manager

Required documents:
- Tracking number
- Proof of item value
- Photos if damaged
- Delivery record
- Claim form
