# SOP: Delivery Priority

Priority order:
1. Medical and temperature-sensitive deliveries
2. Paid express delivery
3. Business account parcels with SLA
4. Standard parcels
5. Failed delivery reattempts

Delay handling:
- If delay is less than 24 hours, customer service may provide updated ETA.
- If delay is more than 24 hours, create delay ticket.
- If delay is more than 48 hours for express delivery, offer service fee refund review.
- If parcel is marked lost, transfer to Claims team.

Failed delivery:
- First reattempt is free.
- Second reattempt may have fee depending on account type.
- Parcel returns to sender after 3 failed delivery attempts.
