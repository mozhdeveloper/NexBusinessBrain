# Logistics FAQ

Q: When should a delay ticket be created?
A: If delay is more than 24 hours.

Q: When can express delivery be reviewed for service fee refund?
A: If delayed more than 48 hours.

Q: Is damaged parcel eligible for claim?
A: Yes, if reported within 24 hours with photos.

Q: Who approves a PHP 12,000 claim?
A: Operations Manager.

Q: What happens after 3 failed delivery attempts?
A: Parcel returns to sender.
