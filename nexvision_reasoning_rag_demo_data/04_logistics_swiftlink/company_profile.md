# SwiftLink Logistics - Company Profile

SwiftLink Logistics is a mid-sized logistics company handling parcel delivery, business cargo, same-day delivery, and warehouse dispatch.

Departments:
- Customer Service
- Dispatch
- Warehouse
- Fleet Operations
- Claims
- Billing

Common business problems:
- Customers ask repetitive questions about delays, lost parcels, and claims.
- Dispatch team needs quick answers on priority rules.
- Claims team needs consistent eligibility decisions.
- Managers spend time checking status manually.

Goal of AI Assistant:
Help customer service, claims, and dispatch teams answer faster using shipment rules, claims policy, and parcel status data.
