# Hospital FAQ

Q: Can an emergency patient be treated without complete billing documents?
A: Yes. Emergency care must start immediately. Billing follows after stabilization.

Q: How long does refund processing take?
A: 7 to 15 business days after complete documents are submitted.

Q: Who approves a PHP 12,000 refund?
A: Finance Manager.

Q: Can a no-show diagnostic appointment be refunded?
A: No, unless cancellation was made 24 hours before the schedule.

Q: What documents are needed for admission?
A: Valid ID, doctor's admission order, deposit or HMO approval, signed consent forms, and emergency contact details.
