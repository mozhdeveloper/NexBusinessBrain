# SOP: Billing and Refund

Refund eligibility:
1. Duplicate payment: eligible for refund after verification.
2. Cancelled procedure: eligible if cancellation is made at least 24 hours before schedule.
3. Unused deposit: eligible after final bill reconciliation.
4. HMO denial after service: not eligible for refund if services were already rendered.
5. No-show diagnostic appointment: non-refundable unless cancellation was made 24 hours before schedule.

Approval rules:
- Below PHP 5,000: Billing Supervisor
- PHP 5,001 to PHP 30,000: Finance Manager
- Above PHP 30,000: Hospital Administrator

Refund processing time:
7 to 15 business days after complete documents are submitted.

Required documents:
- Official receipt
- Valid ID
- Refund request form
- Proof of duplicate or cancellation, if applicable
