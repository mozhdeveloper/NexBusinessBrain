# SOP: Patient Admission

Admission requirements:
- Valid government ID
- Doctor's admission order
- Initial deposit or HMO approval
- Signed consent forms
- Emergency contact details

Admission priority:
1. Emergency patients
2. Scheduled surgery patients
3. Direct admission from outpatient consultation
4. Room upgrade requests

HMO rule:
If patient is using HMO, admission can proceed only after LOA or initial approval is verified, except emergency cases.

Emergency exception:
Emergency patients must receive immediate care even if billing documents are incomplete. Billing team will coordinate after stabilization.

Recommended staff response:
"We will assist you with the admission process. For faster processing, please prepare your ID, doctor's order, HMO approval if applicable, and emergency contact information."
