# MedNova Medical Center - Company Profile

MedNova Medical Center is a mid-sized private hospital with outpatient clinics, emergency care, diagnostics, and inpatient rooms.

Departments:
- Patient Services
- Billing
- Admissions
- Emergency Department
- Laboratory and Diagnostics
- Medical Records
- HR and Training

Common business problems:
- Patients ask repetitive questions about requirements, billing, and schedules.
- Staff need fast access to admission rules and patient handling procedures.
- Delays happen because information is scattered across departments.

Goal of AI Assistant:
Help staff answer patient questions, guide admissions, explain billing requirements, and reduce repetitive calls.
