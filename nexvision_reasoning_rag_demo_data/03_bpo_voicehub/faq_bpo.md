# BPO FAQ

Q: When should an agent escalate a call?
A: When the customer asks for a supervisor more than twice, threatens legal action, reports fraud, becomes abusive after warning, or requires approval beyond agent authority.

Q: What is the passing quality score?
A: 85%.

Q: What happens if an agent scores below 85% for two consecutive weeks?
A: The agent must receive coaching from the Team Leader.

Q: Is tracking update enough reason to escalate?
A: No. Basic tracking updates should be handled by the agent.

Q: What is an automatic fail?
A: Wrong policy information, skipping verification, rude language, unauthorized promise, or failure to document high-risk call.
