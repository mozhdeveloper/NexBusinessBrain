# SOP: Quality Score

Agent quality score is based on:
- Greeting and verification: 15%
- Accuracy of information: 30%
- Empathy and tone: 20%
- Resolution process: 25%
- Documentation: 10%

Passing score:
85%

Automatic fail conditions:
- Sharing wrong policy information
- Skipping identity verification
- Rude or disrespectful language
- Unauthorized promise to customer
- Failure to document high-risk call

Coaching rule:
Agents scoring below 85% for two consecutive weeks must receive coaching from the Team Leader.
