# VoiceHub CX Solutions - Company Profile

VoiceHub CX Solutions is a mid-sized BPO company serving e-commerce, fintech, and healthcare accounts.

Departments:
- Operations
- Training
- Quality Assurance
- Workforce Management
- Client Success
- HR

Common business problems:
- New agents ask repeated questions during live calls.
- Supervisors spend too much time answering process questions.
- Agents give inconsistent answers if knowledge base is outdated.
- Training time is long and expensive.

Goal of AI Assistant:
Support agents with instant answers, call-handling scripts, escalation rules, and account-specific knowledge.
