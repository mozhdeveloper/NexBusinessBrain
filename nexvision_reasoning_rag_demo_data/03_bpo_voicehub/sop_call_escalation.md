# SOP: Call Escalation

Escalate the call if:
1. Customer asks for supervisor more than twice.
2. Customer threatens legal action.
3. Customer reports fraud, account takeover, or unauthorized transaction.
4. Customer is verbally abusive after one warning.
5. Agent does not have authority to approve request.

Do not escalate if:
- Customer only asks for tracking update.
- Customer asks for basic refund status.
- Customer requests simple account information already available in CRM.

Escalation level:
- Level 1: Team Leader
- Level 2: Operations Manager
- Level 3: Client Success Manager

Recommended agent phrase:
"I understand your concern. To make sure this is handled properly, I will escalate this to the right support level."
