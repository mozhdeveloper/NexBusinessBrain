# BuildWise Engineering Solutions - Company Profile

BuildWise Engineering Solutions is a mid-sized construction and engineering company handling commercial fit-outs, warehouse construction, and renovation projects.

Departments:
- Project Management
- Procurement
- Engineering
- Safety and Compliance
- Finance and Billing
- Client Relations

Common business problems:
- Project managers spend too much time searching for specs and approvals.
- Site engineers ask repeated questions about variation orders and safety rules.
- Clients ask for project status updates.
- Delays happen when documents are not easy to find.

Goal of AI Assistant:
Help staff find project rules, safety procedures, approval requirements, billing status, and client update templates faster.
