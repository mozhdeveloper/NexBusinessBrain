# SOP: Site Safety and Incident Handling

All workers and visitors must wear PPE before entering the site.

Required PPE:
- Safety helmet
- Safety shoes
- Reflective vest
- Gloves
- Eye protection when needed

Incident handling:
1. Stop work immediately in the affected area.
2. Give first aid if needed.
3. Report to Safety Officer within 15 minutes.
4. Safety Officer must file an incident report within 24 hours.
5. Work resumes only after clearance from Safety Officer and Project Manager.

High-risk incidents:
- Fall from height
- Electrical shock
- Equipment accident
- Structural cracks
- Fire or gas leak

If high-risk incident occurs, notify Operations Manager and Engineering Head immediately.
