# Construction FAQ

Q: Can site work proceed without client approval?
A: No. Any variation, design change, or cost change requires client approval first.

Q: Who approves a PHP 75,000 variation order?
A: Operations Manager.

Q: Who approves a PHP 150,000 variation order?
A: COO.

Q: What if the variation affects structural safety?
A: Engineering Head must review regardless of cost.

Q: What should staff do after a safety incident?
A: Stop work, give first aid if needed, report to Safety Officer within 15 minutes, and file incident report within 24 hours.
