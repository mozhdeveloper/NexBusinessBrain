# SOP: Project Variation Orders

A variation order is required when there is any change in scope, materials, timeline, labor cost, or approved design.

Approval rules:
1. Below PHP 25,000: Project Manager approval is enough.
2. PHP 25,001 to PHP 100,000: Operations Manager approval is required.
3. Above PHP 100,000: COO approval is required.
4. Any variation affecting structural safety must be reviewed by the Engineering Head regardless of cost.
5. No work may proceed without signed client approval.

Required documents:
- Variation request form
- Cost estimate
- Updated project timeline
- Client approval
- Internal approval based on amount

Recommended response to client:
"Thank you for confirming the requested change. We will prepare the updated cost and timeline for your approval before proceeding."
